Founder- CUSucceed
(Recognized with India Leadership Award by the prestigious Blindwink organization)

Shrikant Sundergiri is a Motivational speaker, life Coach, and Corporate Trainer. He has been working in this field for the last 12 years.

Trained and coached 50,000+ people across India to date. He has taken many CSR projects to encourage women and youth to come forward and pursue their goals because "He believes if you decide something in life, you can surely achieve it."
