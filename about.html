EMP

Assessments Carried out

1st
Psychometric Test

Our training program includes a Psychometric Test designed to assess and enhance key personal attributes such as confidence, communication skills, and emotional intelligence. This test helps identify individual strengths and areas for growth, ensuring a well-rounded development experience.

2nd
Online Communication Test

Our training program features an Online Communication Test powered by AI tools, designed to evaluate communication skills with zero human intervention. This approach minimizes human error and ensures an unbiased assessment of your abilities, helping you improve effectively and accurately.

3rd
Skill Gap Analysis

Our program includes a comprehensive Skill Gap Analysis to identify the difference between a student's current skills and the skills needed to succeed in the future. This helps us create a personalized development plan to bridge the gap effectively.

4th
Train-Test(Mock Drill)-Retrain

We follow a Train-Test-Retrain approach, where we provide focused training, conduct mock tests to assess progress, and, if there are any setbacks, offer additional support and guidance to ensure continuous improvement and success.
